Overview

    This concept project is a branching scenario intended for an online exhibition at Museo Officina dell'Educazione (MODE) in Bologna. The online activity takes the learner through a "walk" and listens to personal stories of women suffering from domestic violence, particularly during the pandemic. I used Twine during the planning phase of the branching of the story, before the final execution in Articulate Storyline 360. Also, I added Javascript so that the online activity gives the learner a personalized certificate at the end.
